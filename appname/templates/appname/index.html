Why Choose Life & Limb?

Life and Limb is a charity focused on providing prosthetic limbs and support to amputees. The organization aims to restore mobility and independence for individuals who have lost limbs due to injury, illness, or congenital conditions. By offering access to high-quality prosthetics and rehabilitation services, Life and Limb helps improve the quality of life for its recipients. Additionally, the charity often engages in advocacy and awareness efforts to highlight the needs of amputees and promote inclusivity in society.
